Why Choose V.O.L

At VOL, students always come first. Our approach to knowledge development starts with partnerships in programme development that builds on extensive networks to develop empowering and life changing curriculum. Our programmes are developed, led and reviewed by seasoned experts from industry and academia. We do not seek to teach nor train, but to be your trusted knowledge development partner.

Learning Model

Our model gives the much needed convenience and affordability. After all, knowledge that empowers and transforms has to be conveniently affordable. For that reason, at VOL you can learn in the comfort of your home, office, or anywhere. Our practical sessions are designed to meet your limited time needs.

We have a longstanding dedication to our students through partnerships in knowledge development, empowerment, and their transformation into thoughtful, caring and agile leaders. At VOL, we are dedicated to providing our students with an intimate, engaged learning environment, continuous individualized support and mentorship.

V.O.L Student

As a student at VOL, you are a valued member of our community. We promise to partner you to make sure your experience with us prepares you not only for professional challenges and success, but for meaningful contributions to the global society and a fulfilling life.

VOL provides both challenging and exciting life experiences. Although challenging, you won’t be alone. We are with you every step of the way—from the moment you apply to the day you graduate and beyond.

At VOL, students always come first, and that comes to life in our programs. You’ll enjoy small class sizes that encourage more conversation, more sharing, and more support from fellow students and facilitators.

Our online learning platforms, combined with customized face to face tutorials make connecting with your studies simple. Benefit from mobile access to your coursework, instant messaging and community tools, a shared blogging platform, and more.
